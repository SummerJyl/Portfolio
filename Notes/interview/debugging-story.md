The "STAR" Method Note: This is a perfect story for the "Tell me about a time you hit a technical roadblock" question.

Situation: Portfolio went to a "White Screen" right before a deployment.

Task: Identify the source of the crash and restore asset visibility.

Action: Used browser dev tools (F12) to trace a ReferenceError, identified a syntax break in a data array, and refactored asset pathing for GitHub Pages environments.

Result: Restored 100% functionality and improved the UX by simplifying the contact method to a direct email link.
